Croquetas

Description

Okay... these chorizo croquetas are a real show stopper. They're crsipy, gooey, and packed with flavour packed chorizo. They come together in less than a couple of hours, and you can easily up this batch for your next party! Let's just say your guests will thank you.

Ingredients

  • 1 dry cured chorizo
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 50g Manchego cheese
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • Panco for breading

Steps

  1. Start by chopping your onion and adding to your pan with some oil. Let them lightly cook down for about 10 minutes.
  2. Once your onions begin to brown, slowly add 1 cup of flor (in small parts) to the pan until it is absorbed into the onions.
  3. Once flour is added, add in milk in small parts until 1 cup is added to the pan. It will boil, then coagulate into the flour and onion mixture until you have a creamy filling.
  4. Take your pan off the heat and mix in grated manchego and chopped cured chorizo.
  5. Add your filling to a pan, cover with plastic wrap, and let it cool in the fridge for about an hour, or until completely cold.
  6. Once cooled, removed mixture and use your hands to form it into small round ovals. Bread them using flour, then egg, then panco, and st onto a plate.
  7. Heat oil and lightly fry croquetas in small batches. Serve warm!
