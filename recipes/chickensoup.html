Chicken Soup

Description

Feeling fall set in? This homemade chicken noodle soup is perfect for the cool nights, and you can use whatever you have in your pantry to spice it up a little bit.

Ingredients

  • Leftover rotissery chicken
  • 3 medium potatoes
  • 2 large carrots
  • 2 stocks of celery
  • 1 can of corn
  • 2 onions

Steps

  1. Carve the remaining chicken from last nights rotissery and seat meat aside. In a large pot, add the chicken carus and fill with water. Let boil on medium for 2-3 hours.
  2. Once the chicken stock is finished, use tongs to remove the carcus and any parts that fell. In the stock, add some water if needed and add in diced potatos, onions and chopped carrots. Let boil for around 30 minutes.
  3. Add in celery and garlic for another 10 minutes.
  4. Once finished boiling, mix in chicken meat, corn and pasta if wanted.
  5. Salt to taste, and servce!
