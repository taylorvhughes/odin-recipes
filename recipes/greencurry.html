Green Curry

Description

This green curry is the bets you'll ever come across - and you can make it in your own kitchen! It's creamy, spicy, and full of nutrients. This recipe will make 3 servings.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs, ~6-8
  • Coconut milk (Thai Rooster is the best!)
  • Onions, 1.5 large
  • Curry paste
  • Curry powder
  • Fresh spinach
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Sear your chicken thighs in oil, roughly 3-5 minutes to give them a nice brown sear.
  2. Remove chicken thighs from the pan and add in onions. Let cook for approx. 5 mins.
  3. Once the onions are soft, add in garlic. Let cook until you can smell it.
  4. Once garlic is fragrant, add in 1 can of coconut milk and one can of water.
  5. Add in the chicken thighs, and let come to a boil. Once boidling, transfer to overn, uncovered, for 45 minutes to an huor.
  6. Top with spinach and serve over rice!
